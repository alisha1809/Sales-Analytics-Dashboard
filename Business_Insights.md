# Business Insights

## Sales Performance

- Technology products generated the highest revenue.
- West region contributed significantly to total sales.
- Corporate customers generated higher average order values.

## Customer Insights

- Consumer segment placed the highest number of orders.
- Repeat customers contributed a major share of revenue.

## Product Insights

- A small number of products generated most of the revenue.
- High discounts reduced profit margins for some categories.

## Recommendations

- Focus marketing on high-performing regions.
- Reduce excessive discounts on low-profit products.
- Improve inventory planning for top-selling products.
- Increase cross-selling opportunities for premium customers.
